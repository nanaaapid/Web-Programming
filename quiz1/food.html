Local Foods

Kare Rajungan



A traditional Tuban dish made from blue crab (rajungan) cooked in rich and savory curry spices, offering a unique coastal flavor.

Becek Menthok



A signature Tuban dish made with duck (menthok) in a thick, spicy black broth similar to rawon, but richer and more flavorful.

Dumbek



A sweet traditional snack made from rice flour, grated coconut, palm sugar, and coconut milk, wrapped in young coconut leaves into a cone shape.

Ampo



A unique Tuban delicacy made from baked clay, traditionally believed to have health benefits and often consumed as a rare snack.
