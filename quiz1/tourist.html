This is our
Tourism Spot

Pantai Boom



The most famous beach in Tuban, located near the town square. It is a historical port area from the Majapahit era and now a popular tourist destination.

Pantai Remen



A beautiful white sand beach located in Jenu District, Tuban. Known for its lagoon and calm atmosphere, often called Tuban’s “mini Bali.”

Goa Akbar



A large natural cave located in the center of Tuban city, near the town square. It features impressive stalactites and stalagmites and is easily accessible.

Kwan Sing Bio



The largest Chinese temple in Southeast Asia located by the sea. Famous for its giant crab statue and unique coastal architecture.

Masjid Agung Tuban



A grand mosque located in the Tuban town square. It combines traditional Javanese and Islamic architecture and is one of the city’s landmarks.

Museum Kambang Putih



A museum that preserves historical artifacts from the Majapahit era and Tuban’s cultural heritage. Located near the town square.
